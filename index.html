Ares Excursion

Embark on a journey to Mars

Experience the thrill of an interstellar adventure as you explore the wonders of Mars on this trip. Witness the colossal Olympus Mons, the largest volcano in the solar system. Explore the the magnificent Valles Marineris, the largest and deepest valley. Then, pay homage to space exploration history as we visit the landing sites of NASA’s groundbreaking missions: Viking 1 and Sojourner.

Limited seats available for this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity, so reserve your spot now and be among the few to explore the Red Planet up close!

Cronos Flyby

Immerse yourself in the grandeur of Saturn

Fly over the awe-inspiring beauty of Saturn’s magnificent rings. As you venture closer to the planet, prepare to be captivated by the unique splendor of the planet’s hexagonal north pole.

On your return, set your sights on Titan, Saturn’s largest moon, where you’ll witness the unique landscape of methane lakes and ice mountains in passing.

This journey promises an unforgettable experience as you immerse yourself in the mysteries and wonders of the Saturnian system.
